About Us

The 27th session of the Conference of the Parties (COP 27) to the UNFCCC will take place in Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt. COP 27 was originally expected to take place from 8-20 November 2021. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, COP 26 was rescheduled from November 2020 to November 2021. As a result, COP 27 will take place from 7-18 November 2022. We will update this page when the agenda for COP 27 is announced. To receive updates from the SDG Knowledge Hub in your inbox, subscribe to the SDG Update newsletter.
